Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how StareMe ("we," "our," "us," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

The short version: StareMe processes your camera frames entirely on your device to detect blinks. Camera images never leave your phone. The only data we store on our servers is your username, scores, and rank — and only if you choose to sign in.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera data (on-device only)

StareMe uses your device's front-facing camera to detect blinks and look-aways during gameplay. Camera frames are processed entirely on your device in real time and are never uploaded, transmitted, stored, or shared with us or any third party. No images, video, or biometric identifiers leave your phone.

The camera is only active while you are playing a round. You can revoke camera permission at any time in your device settings; without it, the game cannot function.

1.2 Account information (when you sign in)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your Google account user ID
  • The display name you choose to show on the leaderboard

We do not collect your email address or any other Google account data. Sign-in is optional — you can play as a guest without creating an account.

1.3 Gameplay data

When you sign in and play, we store the following on our servers (Firebase Firestore) tied to your user ID:

  • Your high scores per skin
  • Your rating points and rank tier
  • Your chosen display name

Guest players' data is stored only on their device and is never sent to our servers.

1.4 Advertising data

We display rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Your device's advertising ID
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

This data is used by Google to serve and measure ads. See Google's privacy policy for details. You can reset or disable your advertising ID in your device settings.

1.5 Diagnostic information

We may collect crash reports and basic diagnostic information to fix bugs. This data is not linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide game functionality (blink detection, scoring, leaderboards)
  • Sync your progress across devices when you sign in
  • Display rewarded video ads
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Improve the game

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your information. We share information only with:

  • Google Firebase — to store your scores and account data
  • Google AdMob — to serve advertisements
  • Google Sign-In — to authenticate you when you sign in

We do not share information with any other third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Camera frames: Never retained. Processed in memory and discarded.
  • Account data: Retained while your account exists. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 7).
  • Guest data: Stored only on your device until you uninstall the app.

5. Children's Privacy

StareMe is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Security

We use Google Firebase's standard security practices to protect data in transit and at rest. All network communication uses HTTPS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

To request account deletion, email us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Regional Notices

India (DPDP Act)

If you are a user in India, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 applies. You have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal data, and to nominate another individual to exercise these rights on your behalf in case of incapacity. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR)

We rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent for advertising and analytics, collected via the Google UMP consent flow on first launch
  • Legitimate interest for diagnostics and core game functionality
  • Contract performance for account features when you sign in

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

California (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Delete StareMe Account & Data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  1. In the app: Open Stare.Me → tap your profile icon (top right) → "Delete account & data" → confirm. This removes your Google-linked profile, scores, ranks, and referrals immediately.
  2. By email: If you no longer have the app installed, email staremeplay@gmail.com from the Google account you signed in with. We will delete your account and confirm within 30 days.

What gets deleted: Google email, display name, profile photo URL, all game scores, rank progress, and referral records. The app does not store camera frames — face detection runs on-device only.

What is retained: No personal data is retained after deletion. Anonymous, aggregated leaderboard statistics may persist with no link to you.
